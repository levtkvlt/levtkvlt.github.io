December 25, 2022

Merry Christmas, whatever that means to you. I merely see it as a designated time to show the people I love how much I care about them. But of course, the time is ripe to take petty shots at Christians.

LevtKvlt's first physical releases, Arra (limited edition cassette and handmade-to-order cassette), have been released today. Coming up next is "Total Absence of Light" by Devour the Creation, and "Allesomvattende Verscheuring" by Profetische Waanzinshymne. Both will have limited and handemade-to-order editions, as will most (if not all) LevtKvlt releases.

Bauru, Brazil's Devour the Creation self-released "Total Absence of Light" on August 13, 2022 via Bandcamp. In two tracks spanning 22 minutes, Antagonist blends harsh noise, dungeon synth, and unapologeticly raw black metal to create a eulogy for the world. A true product of chaos.

Next will be a brand new artist from The Netherlands named Profetische Waanzinshymne. Their debut release, "Allesomvattende Verscheuring", is a refreshing take on techno. The artist replaces typical edm sounds, time signatures and song structures with unforgiving noise and a void of concern for convention.

Both of these releases can be expected in January, 2023.

- Deitritus
