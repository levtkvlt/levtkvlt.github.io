      • We are artists in capitalism, not capitalists in art. You decide what this is worth to you, relative to your means.

      • Unspecified donations contribute to shipping costs and future offerings.
      • Your personal information will never be shared by LevtKvlt under any cirumstances and discarded upon shipment.
      • Disingenuous requests will be ignored and refunded.
